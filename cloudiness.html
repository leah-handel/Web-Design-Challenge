Cloud Cover

In this figure, we see measurements of cloud cover that seem to form bands across latitudes. The bands are most likely artifacts of measurements that are recorded as human estimations of cloud cover and are probably not more precise than 5% increments. Cloud cover does not seem strongly correlated to latitude but probably is related to humidity.
