Summary: Lattitude vs. Weather

Welcome to Weather! We analyzed the relationship between lattitude and temperature, humidity, cloud cover, and wind speed to find variations in weather around the world. Click on any visualization to the right to learn more.

Weather! uses data from OpenWeather's API for over 500 cities around the world. Data was pulled on January 5th, 2017 and reflects the current weather conditions at that time. Figures were created using matplotlib.
